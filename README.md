# 👋 Hi, I'm Rekhi

I am interested in computational neuroscience and machine learning, focusing on how intelligent behavior emerges from neural systems, data, and learning algorithms.

My work lies at the intersection of brain-inspired computation, deep learning, and neural data analysis. I explore how principles from biological neural systems can inform artificial models, and how machine learning can be used to understand complex neural activity.

## What I Work On

- Neural data modeling (e.g., spike trains, behavioral datasets such as the Steinmetz dataset)
- Feature extraction from high-dimensional neural signals
- Adaptive and self-improving learning systems
- Experiment tracking and reproducible machine learning pipelines
- Bridging neuroscience and artificial intelligence

## Current Focus

- Building learning systems inspired by neural computation
- Understanding representation learning in biological and artificial networks
- Designing efficient classifiers for neural datasets
- Developing structured experiment tracking systems using SQLite

## Research Direction

I am particularly interested in the question:

How can we design systems that learn, adapt, and generalize in ways similar to biological brains?
